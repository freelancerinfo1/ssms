Our Goal

Sri Sevugamoorthy School on a pedestal of emote today has a mission statement which is inspired by a dream to save society, planting seeds of patriotism, provide holistic education in assimilating new frontiers of knowledge and discover the countries of skills and competencies in the guest for excellence, anchoring as valuable instruments of service to the nation.

Like a sculptor who chisels stones into beautiful statues we nurture kids for a better tomorrow.

Our primary aim is to foster in students a spirit of enquiry, develop the essential qualities of honesty, trust, integrity, courage and instill self-efficacy and self-competitive world and equip them with positive behavior, knowledge, leadership skills and an understanding of human values and ethics. We need to bring about a good society in which all human beings can live happily in peace, without violence, with security. The school provides a pleasing learning environment with a spirit of caring and sharing and with closed bonds between teachers and students.
