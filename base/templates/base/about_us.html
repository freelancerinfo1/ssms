Sri Sevugamoorthy Matric School at Singampunari, owned and managed by The Sri Sevugamoorthy Educational Trust, is one of the leading most sought after senior matriculation school in Singampunari imparting quality education at affordable cost .Our vision is to “Educate, Enlighten, and Empower” has been tested over the last 8 years, and has proved to be effective and successful time and again, accepted and appreciated by community. The school which had a humble beginning in 2010 today has got more than 1000 students and 70 staff members on roll. As we completed 8 years of yeomen service, what comes to my mind is that “Success comes to those who work hard and stays with those, who don’t rest on the laurels of the past” We live today in a world that is so very different from the one we grew up in, the one we were educated in .The world today is changing at such on accelerated rate and we as educators need to pause and reflect on this entire system of education are our schools well equipped to prepare our children to face the challenges that the future holds questions such as this are factors that motivate us to go through a continuous process of reflection and hence we have done Sri Sevugamoorthy academy work at implementing a well – balanced curriculum to ensure that the children who walk into the portals of our school will not just love their school years but truly be prepares them to face life‘s changes. It was Margaret Mead who said “Children must be taught how to think, not what to think”



Dear parents and students,
As the Principal of SS Matric School, my primary focus is upgrade the knowledge of my students. Student must know how to think, to problem solve, to innovate and to collaborate. All students must be prepared to be successful in the 21st century careers many of which do not even exist yet. Schooling is our first contact with the world, a period of joy, healthy competition, fearing, adjustment, sharing and love. Our philosophy of education has always been meeting the needs of the individual child. Children acquire skills and knowledge easily if we can make the surrounding stimulating and purposeful. We try to understand and work with each child and we firmly believe that it is our responsibility to enable the child in our care to develop as a whole. It’s not the wide open spaces, classrooms, the library laboratories are the large fields that make the school. It is in the heart of the students and staffs that the true institution exists. According to academic analysts, the youth of future generation are going to be more prone to violence and deviant behavior. This deteriorating trend is a matter of great concern to parents as well as teachers
